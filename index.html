About The Nail Bar

Enjoy meticulous manicures & pedicures in a relaxing space where quality meets care. Tools sterilized & stations sanitized after every client.

Meet Sumesh Moodley

Hello! I'm Sumesh, the founder and lead nail technician at The Nail Bar. What started as a passion for nail art has blossomed into a home-based studio where I provide professional nail services to clients across Midrand.

I specialize in creating beautiful, long-lasting nail enhancements that are tailored to each client's unique style. My home studio offers a comfortable, private environment where you can relax while I work my magic on your nails.

With continuous training in the latest techniques and trends, I ensure that every client leaves with nails they love and a smile on their face.
